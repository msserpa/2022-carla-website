Advanced Computing Trends

About the workshop

The workshop on Advanced Computing Trends in Latin America aims to provide a space to share high-quality original research contributions on various aspects of advanced computing, e-science, disruptive computing, applications of HPC and Advanced Computing, quantum computing, large scale and collaborative computing architectures, data-science and HPC trends in the latinoamerican region.

The workshop focuses on Latin America developments, however other contributions are possible to present the different topics: education, HPC/AI applications, Green HPC, applied HPC (smart cities, agricultural, social, culture, healthcare, climate exchange, security), quantum computing, non-von neumann architectures and related topics.

Chairs:

  • Javier Montoya (University of Cartagena, Colombia)
  • Isidoro Gitler (ABACUS CINVESTAV-IPN, Mexico)
