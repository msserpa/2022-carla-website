Panel - HPC Collaboration between Asia and Latin America

Agenda | Thursday September 29th, 2022

Time of Brazil (GMT -3)

  • 13:30 - 13:50
    Prof. Toshihiro Hanawa, Information Technology Center, The University of Tokyomins Representative from NII or related organization.
  • 13:50 - 14:10
    Louis Hyunho Choi, Executive Officer / Ph. D(Candidate, Business Management), Trans-Eurasia Information Network Cooperation Centre (TEIN*CC) Representative from ARNET or related organization.
  • 14:10 - 14:30
    Prof. Jianping Wu, Department of Computer Science at Tsinghua University and a member of Chinese Academy of Engineering.
  • 14:30 - 14:50
    Patricio Rey, General Manager, Desarrollo País, Chile.
  • 14:50 - 15:06
    Round of questions to participants.
Objective of the panel:

To present actions that are being developed in Asia and Latin America and explore lines of activities that foster the collaboration between high computer centers and national networks in both regions. During the session, each participant will have 20 minutes to present the initiatives and actions developed in their region. During the final 30 minutes, the moderator will ask questions to each one of them, trying to focus on the next steps and possible joint initiatives. A preparation meeting between participants and the moderator would take place in advance.

Moderator:
  • Luis Eliércer Cadenas, Executive Director, RedCLARA
