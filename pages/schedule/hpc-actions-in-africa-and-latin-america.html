Panel - HPC actions in Africa and Latin America

Agenda | Tuesday September 27th, 2022

Time of Brazil (GMT -3)

  • 14:00 - 14:20
    Prof. Maïssa MBAYE, Associate Professor at Gaston Berger University of Saint-Louis in Senegal and Coordinator of the African Center of Excellence in Mathematics, Informatics and ICT (ACE MITIC)
  • 14:20 - 14:40
    Representative from Ubuntunet Alliance (TBC)
  • 14:40 - 15:00
    Carla Osthoff (TBC), Researcher at National Laboratory for Scientific Computing (LNCC-Brazil)
  • 15:00 - 15:30
    Round of questions to participants about next steps/possible collaborative actions
Objective of the panel:

To present actions that are being developed in Africa and Latin America and explore lines of actions that foster the collaboration between high computer centers and national networks in both regions.

Moderator:
  • Luis Eliércer Cadenas, Executive Director, RedCLARA
